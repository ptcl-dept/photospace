Package (.zip) bakes the current photo at full resolution — guided-filter depth upsample, sky/edge masks, world normals — and downloads the same 5-file package photospace-cli bake and photospace-runtime read (photo + depth.png/mask.png/normal.png/meta.json). The photo is encoded as AVIF where supported, falling back to WebP/PNG (recorded in meta.json). Edge suppression keeps the effect from bleeding across depth discontinuities (silhouettes).
